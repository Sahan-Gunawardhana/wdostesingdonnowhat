A Taste of the Wild Coast

Indulge in a culinary journey at Wild Coast Tented Lodge

The dining experience at the Wild Coast Tented Lodge is a truly unique one. The restaurants are located in stunning settings, either overlooking the Indian Ocean or the Yala National Park. The food is fresh and delicious, and the service is impeccable. Two of the most popular events at the tented lodge restaurants are the sundowners and the jungle afternoon tea. Sundowners are a leisurely affair, where guests can enjoy drinks and snacks while watching the sunset over the ocean. Jungle afternoon tea is a more formal affair, where guests are treated to a traditional Sri Lankan tea service in the middle of the jungle. Whether you are looking for a romantic evening with your loved one or a special occasion to celebrate, the dining experience at the Wild Coast Tented Lodge is sure to exceed your expectations.

The Pavilion

The Dining Pavilion at the Wild Coast Tented Lodge serves a variety of Sri Lankan and international dishes made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. The menu changes seasonally, but you can always expect to find delicious and unique dishes. Some of the signature dishes include kiribath, a traditional Sri Lankan dish made with milk rice, coconut milk, and spices; sambar, a lentil stew that is a popular side dish in Sri Lanka; kottu, a stir-fry dish that is made with roti, vegetables, and meat or seafood; egg hoppers, a type of pancake that is made with eggs and coconut milk; and Sri Lankan curry, a variety of curries that are made with different meats, vegetables, and spices. The Dining Pavilion is a great place to enjoy a delicious meal while relaxing in the beautiful surroundings of the Yala National Park.

The Ten Tuskers Bar

The Ten Tuskers Bar is a luxurious open-air bar located at the Wild Coast Tented Lodge in Yala, Sri Lanka. It is named after the ten elephants that are often seen roaming the nearby Yala National Park. The bar is situated alongside the resort's infinity pool, and it offers stunning views of the Indian Ocean. The interior of the bar is decorated in a stylish and contemporary style, with bamboo chandeliers, leather seating, and copper accents. The bar offers a wide selection of cocktails, wines, and spirits, as well as a menu of light bites. It is also a popular spot for sundowners, as the views of the ocean are simply breathtaking at sunset.

The Sunset Bar

The Sunset Bar is a relaxed and informal bar located at the Wild Coast Tented Lodge in Yala, Sri Lanka. It is located in a secluded spot on the edge of the jungle, with stunning views of the sunset over the Indian Ocean. The bar is decorated in a rustic and natural style, with bamboo furniture and thatched roofs. The menu features a wide selection of cocktails, wines, and beers, as well as a small selection of light snacks. The Sunset Bar is the perfect place to relax and unwind after a day of exploring the national park. It is also a popular spot for watching the sunset over the ocean.
