Welcome to our tented lodge on the outskirts of Yala National Park. Here where nature meets luxury, you'll find a blend of comfort and wilderness.
As the canvas wall bring you closer to nature, we invite you to immerse yourself in the simplicity of you sarroundings. Whether you're here for the excitement of the Safari or the peacfulness of starry nights, we'are delighted to have you as our guess in this unique retreat.

Explore the
Yala National Park

Yala National Park is a lush and sectionerse wilderness home to the country's largest leopard population, elephants, sloth bears, and an awe-inspiring array of vibrant birdlife. Expert guides share their encyclopaedic knowledge and ardent passion for the natural world on safaris and nature walks, allowing you to connect with Yala's unfettered heart and learn about its conservation.

Experience the Sri Lankan
Culture at Our Lodge

At our tented lodge, discover a subtle yet captivating cultural ambiance:

  • Guided Experiences: Expert-led safaris and walks reveal Yala's wildlife wonders, guided by rangers who share their deep connection with the land.
  • Nature and Comfort: Embrace modern comfort within nature's embrace, where each element complements the other.

Cultural threads weave through our lodge:

  • Local Insights: Engage with our staff, gaining glimpses of local traditions and ways of life.
  • Fireside Connections: Gather by the fire, sharing stories and forging connections under the night sky.
  • Sustainable Spirit: Reflecting our commitment to Yala's preservation, embrace eco-friendly practices. Amid this delicate interplay of culture and conservation, our tented lodge offers an authentic Yala experience.

Immerse Yourself in the Wild
Create memories under the stars

  1. Game drives: Go on a game drive and see some of the amazing animals that call Yala National Park home, including elephants, leopards, and sloth bears.
  2. Bird watching: See some of the over 500 species of birds that live in the area, including peacocks, eagles, and owls.
  3. Swimming: Cool off in the pool after a long day of exploring.
  4. Fishing: Try your luck at catching some of the fish that live in the nearby river.
Dine in Style
  1. The Dining Pavilion: Enjoy a delicious meal in our elegant dining room, with views of the surrounding bush.
  2. The Ten Tuskers Bar: Relax with a drink in our cozy bar, with a fireplace and a selection of board games.
  3. Wilderness Dining: Enjoy a gourmet meal under the stars in the heart of the jungle.
  4. Discover Sri Lankan Cuisine: Learn how to cook traditional Sri Lankan dishes from a local chef.
  5. Daily Sundowners: Enjoy cocktails and canapes on the beach as the sun sets.
